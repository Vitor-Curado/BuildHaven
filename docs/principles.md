# Principles

* **Strictly no <div>:** divs are lazy, we are anti-div puritans. We must purge <divs> from these lands.
* **Avoidance of JS:** JavaScript clearly presents an issue and thus it must be replaced by Leptos.
* **Hybrid frontend approach:** Neither fully Askama, neither fully Leptos; somewhere in between playing their strenghts and minimizing their weaknesses. Static content stays with Askama, Reactive content stays with Leptos
* **No external styling:** cause I want control over my own styling
* **Lean, mean and clean:** no Daddy AWS to take care of me, only the necessities.
